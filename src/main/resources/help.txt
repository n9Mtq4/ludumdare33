The Adventures of Hareeny and Chair
by: n9Mtq4

About:
You are a monster who has been instructed to
go into a spoiled child's house and scare him or her.
Being spoiled, the child has hired guards to catch
scary monsters. Your goal is to navigate and search
the house for the child sleeping in their bed,
while avoiding guards.

Controls:
WASD or arrow keys to move
E to use monster's ability
M to toggle music

Tips:
 - The guards don't like monsters, but the dislike being
   fired more. They wont stray far from their post.
 - The guards can't see well in the dark. Use it to your
   advantage
